极大似然法

假设连续$X$满足Logistic Distribution，则分布概率和密度函数分别为：

$$
\begin{align}
F(x) &= \frac{1}{1+e^{-(x-\mu)/\gamma}} \\
f(x) &= F’(x) = \frac{e^{-(x-\mu)/\gamma}}{\gamma(1+e^{-(x-\mu)/\gamma})^2}
\end{align}
$$
